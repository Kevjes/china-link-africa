# Prompts de Génération d'Images - China Link Africa

Utilisez ces prompts avec des outils comme Midjourney, DALL-E 3 ou Stable Diffusion pour générer les assets visuels du site.

## 1. Identité Visuelle (Logo)
**Prompt :**
> Professional, modern, and minimalist logo for a brand named "China Link Africa". The design should abstractly represent a bridge, connection, or handshake between the map of Africa and the map of China.
> **Style :** Vector, Flat Design, Corporate.
> **Colors :** Primary Red (#DE2910), Accent Gold (#FFDE00), and Dark Onyx/Black.
> **Vibe :** Trust, dynamism, educational excellence, and international business.

## 2. Page d'Accueil (Home)
### Hero Section
**Prompt :**
> Cinematic wide shot of the Shanghai skyline or Beijing CBD at sunrise. In the foreground, a silhouette or back view of a diverse group of students and business professionals (African and Asian) looking towards the city skyline.
> **Lighting :** Warm golden hour sunlight, hopeful and inspiring atmosphere.
> **Style :** High-end architectural photography, photorealistic, 4k, highly detailed.
> **Vibe :** Opportunity, Future, Connection.

### Section Confiance/Témoignage (Nouveau)
**Prompt :**
> Close-up portrait of a happy African student smiling confidently, holding books, standing in front of a modern university library in China. Soft focus background.
> **Style :** Commercial portrait, high quality, natural lighting.
> **Vibe :** Success, Happiness, Trust.

## 3. Page Bourses (Scholarships)
### Header Bourses
**Prompt :**
> A diverse group of university students walking together on a modern, beautiful Chinese university campus paths lined with trees. Traditional chinese architecture mixed with modern buildings in background.
> **Lighting :** Bright daylight, soft shadows.
> **Vibe :** Academic success, educational journey.

### Illustration Catégorie "Université"
**Prompt :**
> Interior shot of a modern, high-tech university lecture hall or laboratory in China, filled with diverse students engaged in learning.
> **Style :** Clean, bright, academic.

## 4. Page Services (Business & Sourcing)
### Sourcing & Achat
**Prompt :**
> Inside a modern, clean logistics warehouse or shipping port in China. Workers in safety vests inspecting cargo containers or boxes. Professional and organized.
> **Vibe :** Industry, Efficiency, Global Trade.

### Voyages d'Affaires
**Prompt :**
> A first-class airplane seat view or a business lounge setting with a laptop and a view of a runway. Or a sleek high-speed train (CRH) moving through a landscape.
> **Vibe :** Premium travel, speed, comfort.

### Logistique Export
**Prompt :**
> A cargo ship at a busy port (like Shanghai or Ningbo) loaded with colorful containers, sunny day, blue sky.
> **Vibe :** Global commerce, scale, capability.

## 5. Page Voyage (Tourism & Visa)
### Grande Muraille
**Prompt :**
> Breathtaking landscape shot of the Great Wall of China winding through green mountains, bathed in morning mist or sunset light. No heavy crowds.
> **Style :** National Geographic style landscape photography.
> **Vibe :** Majestic, History, Adventure.

### Visa & Administration
**Prompt :**
> Close-up flat lay photography of a travel planning desk. Items include: A passport (generic or Chinese visa visible), an airline ticket, a pen, and a small globe.
> **Style :** Professional product photography, shallow depth of field (bokeh), clean desk.
> **Vibe :** Preparation, excitement, professional assistance.

### Culture & Vie
**Prompt :**
> A vibrant street food night market in China (like Chengdu or Xi'an) with colorful lanterns and steam rising from food stalls. Lively atmosphere but not chaotic.
> **Vibe :** Culture, Flavor, Experience.

## 6. Page Contact
### Service Client
**Prompt :**
> A friendly customer support representative (diverse ethnicity) wearing a headset, smiling warmly, sitting in a modern bright office with a map of the world or China in the background.
> **Vibe :** Helpfulness, Accessibility, Human connection.
